Hospitals have plans to keep coronavirus patients separate and supply staff with protective masks and suits.All hospital patients with flu-like symptoms are being tested. If someone tests positive, they may be moved to one of the main hospitals.Patients with mild symptoms are being asked to self-isolate at home.
